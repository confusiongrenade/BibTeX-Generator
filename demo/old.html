Search Hints
To search for an exact phrase, place it in quotation marks
“great depression”
"Civil War"
Combine search terms using AND, OR, NOT (in all CAPS)
plastics AND environment will give results that have both terms
plastics OR environment will give results that have either term or both terms
plastics NOT environment will eliminate results that contain the term environment
Searches can be nested using parentheses
(irish OR ireland) AND "catholic church"
Wild Cards
An asterisk (*) will give you any word which starts with the letters you use
allerg* will bring results for allergy, allergies, allergens, allergic, etc.
A question mark (?) will give you words with any one letter in that position
wom?n will bring results for woman, women, etc.
